Reuse LCA project

The results of the Reuse-LCA project are presented to better understand how reusing components in construction reduces environmental impacts, especially GHG emissions. These results help identify current practices and barriers to reuse, and analyze how design choices and the type of reused materials influence the overall impact of a building. This information is crucial for promoting sustainable construction strategies and reducing the carbon footprint of buildings.
